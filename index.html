Story

  • First Date

    Our first date was at Clubhouse BFD in Rochester Hills. Gabi rushed home from work to meet me right after I got off a plane from California. This is where we realized we shared a love for their sweet potato tater tots and travel. After this first weekend, our lives have never been the same!

  • First Trip

    Our first trip was to Toronto. It was on Valentine's Day & the NBA All-Star game. We may have only been there for a short time, but we wandered all around town and made some amazing memories. To this day, Canada is our Valentine's Day tradition.

  • Engagement

    I (Jon) had this all planned out the moment Gabi told me she was going to Europe with her mom. I picked out a ring, made a little poster with "Will you marry me?" printed on it and grabbed my parents to suprise Gabi at the airport. We even had an extra photographer present!
